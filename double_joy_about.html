About Double Joy Cookies

Inspired by My Girls

It started small — cookies for my own girls' birthdays, decorated late at night because I wanted their day to feel a little more special. Somewhere along the way I realized I was actually good at this, and that maybe it could be something more than a once-a-year birthday project.

My girls — the whole reason this started

That's how Double Joy Cookies came to be. Every set is still made the same way those first ones were — by hand, one at a time, made to fit the celebration it's for.

"Double Joy" means the joy you feel receiving the cookies,
and the joy I feel making them. Happiness shared both ways.

These days that means custom cookies for birthdays, showers, weddings, holidays, and just-because moments — always designed around your event, never off a shelf. I still get the same rush from a finished box that I did with that first batch for my girls.
