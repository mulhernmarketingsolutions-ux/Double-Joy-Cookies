Frequently Asked Questions

The essentials — if you don't see what you need here, reach out directly on the Contact page.

How far in advance should I order?

Custom orders typically need at least 2–3 weeks' notice, especially for larger sets or busy seasons (holidays, wedding season). Reach out as early as you can — I'll always try to accommodate a tighter timeline when possible. Orders placed with less than 2 weeks' notice may incur a $20/dozen rush fee, subject to availability.

Do you ship, or is it pickup only?

The custom order form is set up for local pickup in the Portland, OR area — I'm not able to ship at this time. For local delivery, reach out directly and I'll see what I can work out.

Can you accommodate allergies or dietary restrictions?

Let me know about any allergies or dietary needs when you submit your order, and I'll do my best to accommodate. Please note cookies are made in a home kitchen that also handles common allergens like nuts, dairy, egg, and gluten.

How should I store my cookies, and how long do they stay fresh?

Store cookies in an airtight container at room temperature, away from direct sunlight. They'll stay fresh and beautiful for about 1–2 weeks — full care instructions come with every order.

How does pricing work?

Every set is priced individually based on quantity, design complexity, and finishing details — see the Pricing page for a full breakdown of the four tiers and what drives the price up or down. Send your ideas through the custom order form and I'll follow up with a personal quote.

What if I need gift bows or extra packaging?

Gift bows can be added for +$6/dozen — just let me know on the order form. Every order comes beautifully packaged and ready to gift as-is.

Can I make changes or cancel my order?

Life happens! Reach out as soon as you can if your plans change — see the full Terms & Policies page for details on cancellations and changes.

Still Have Questions?

Reach out directly and I'll get back to you within 24–48 hours.
