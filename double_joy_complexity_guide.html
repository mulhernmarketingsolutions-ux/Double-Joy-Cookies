What Goes Into Every Order

From Simple to Showstopper

No two orders cost the same, because no two orders take the same amount of work. Here's what actually drives the difference — using real sets Emily has made.

1Flat color blocks, minimal blending
2One shape, repeated

01 — Basic

Clean & Simple

Great for everyday orders — a repeated shape, a tight color palette, and confident, clean icing work.

1Hand-piped fine linework
2Custom-cut scalloped shape
3Tiny metallic accent details
4Hand-lettered script
02 — Standard

Custom & Coordinated

A custom-cut shape, hand-lettering, and fine linework — designed as a coordinated set, not a single cookie.

1Dimensional details — real tied bows & tassels
2Hand-painted, wet-on-wet shading
3Many unique shapes, no repeats
4Six-plus coordinated colors
5Miniature appliqué details
03 — Complex

Showstopper

Many unique shapes, hand-painted shading, and dimensional details — every cookie in the set is different.

Most orders land somewhere between Standard and Complex — quantity, timeline, and how many colors/shapes you'd like all factor in too. Share your vision through the order form and Emily will follow up with pricing specific to your design.
